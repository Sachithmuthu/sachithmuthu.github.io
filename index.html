An Innovative Research and Development Engineer well adept at designing, fabricating, testing, and deploying reliable digital products or services

I completed my PhD at the Augmented Human Lab of Auckland Bioengineering Institute at the University of Auckland (UoA), New Zealand. My research interests lie in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Assistive Augmentation, and Haptic Interfaces. I am highly passionate about creating new ideas and coming up with feasible solutions to relevant problems. I am originally from the beautiful island, Sri Lanka, and I completed my honors degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. In 2016, I joined the SUTD-MIT International Design Centre in Singapore as a research assistant. Before joining the UoA, I worked at Synopsys as a Corporate Application Engineer, where I obtained industrial experience with multinational companies. Apart from academic interests, I love sports, food, photography, traveling, and gardening.
