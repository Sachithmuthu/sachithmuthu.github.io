I am a Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher and a Ph.D. candidate at the Augmented Human Lab of Auckland Bioengineering Institute in the University of Auckland, New Zealand. My dissertation focuses on HCI, Assistive Augmentation, and Haptic Interfaces. I enjoy forming new ideas and composing achievable solutions to broadly relevant problems. I completed my honors degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka. Also, I worked as a Research Assistant at the SUTD-MIT International Design Center in Singapore. Just before joining the UoA, I worked at Synopsys as a Corporate Application Engineer. Apart from academic interests, I like sports, food, and traveling.
